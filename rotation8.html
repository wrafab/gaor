Rotation 8: Soldering and Electronics


Soldering is a process in which two or more items are joined together by melting and putting a filler metal having a lower melting point than the adjoining metal.

My first project of soldering is sound-to-light kit. It was not successful because the lights are always on, no matter it received sound or no sound.

My second project is a LED light. It was successful. The blue line blinks really cool.

Overall, the process of soldering was easy and relaxing.
